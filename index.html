Publication

Here is my publication I have done lately. Feel free to check them out.

    Effects of Tapering and Electro‑Osmosis on Copper‑Suspended Nanofluid Through a Composite Stenosed Artery with Permeable Walls: Exact Solutions

    The complex interaction of blood flow in composite stenosed arteries, focusing on the effects of electro-osmosis and nanoparticles. Studies show that blood flow resistance and wall shear stress increase with peripheral layer viscosity. The use of copper nanoparticles reduces blood flow impedance in stenosed arteries, making them potential carriers for targeted drug delivery. Computational simulations reveal how nanoparticle concentration, electric fields, and magnetic effects influence blood velocity, temperature, and shear stress. These findings have implications for enhancing microcirculation, developing novel treatments, and improving cardiovascular health through better stent designs and nanofluid applications in medical therapies.
